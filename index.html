Nationwide, nearly 50,000 bicyclists suffer serious head injuries each year. Three types of brain injuries can happen in a cycling accident concussions, contusions, and hemorrhages. Wearing a helmet can prevent head and brain injuries caused by a bike accident. Vicious Cycles encourage everyone to wear a helmet while riding to be safe.
